    Sponsor Demo and Lightning talk presentations

    What is a Sponsor Demo?

    At the end of each week, we have to make a presentation (four minutes long), about something that we learned that week. It can be a big "AHA" moment, a problem we struggled with, or anything that we feel that it was important or useful during that week.

    What are the lightning talks?

    Every morning, someone does a 10-15 minutes long presentation about a topic, that is not included in the syllabus, but could be useful or interesting, and has something to do with our studies.
